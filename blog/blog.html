About Me:

Hey there! 👋

My name is Valerio, I'm a high school senior (class of 2022) and I run this blog for my own enjoyment.

My main interests lie in STEM fields, mostly computer science and math, although I enjoy learning about any field I know little about, especially linguistics and political science.

Expect badly-written essays about whatever I find noteworthy during a particular period. All opinions are my own, no identification with actual persons (living or deceased), places, buildings, and products is intended or should be inferred, no animals were harmed in the making, all that jazz.

Enjoy! (or don't, it's a free country!)
